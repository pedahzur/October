# Embedded Response Capacity (ERC): Organizational Resilience and Crisis Response on October 7, 2023

## Research question

How did Israeli security personnel achieve an effective military response despite systematic collapse of communication systems and command-and-control infrastructure following the Hamas attack on October 7, 2023?

## Thesis / central argument

I argue that frontline Israeli security units were able to mobilize, coordinate, and act effectively under extreme systemic disruption not because of centralized planning or real-time command, but because of *Embedded Response Capacity* (ERC) — a set of structural, cultural, and operational capabilities (SOF training, reserves experience, officer initiative) that enabled decentralized action when formal C2 systems were degraded or disabled.
