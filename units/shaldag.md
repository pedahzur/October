# Shaldag Unit (Unit 5101)

**Tag:** `unit:Shaldag`
**Branch:** Israeli Air Force (IAF) Special Forces
**Hebrew:** יחידת שלדג

## Overview

Shaldag (Unit 5101) is the Israeli Air Force's primary special operations ground unit. Originally established for terminal guidance of precision air strikes, it evolved into a full-spectrum direct-action and reconnaissance unit. On October 7, Shaldag was one of the first organized IDF special operations forces to respond to the kibbutz attacks, most notably at Kibbutz Be'eri.

## Role on October 7

Shaldag forces were deployed to **Kibbutz Be'eri** as part of the IDF's emergency response. The unit conducted rescue operations under sustained Hamas fire, suffering multiple KIA in the process. The Be'eri operation became one of the most detailed and documented special forces engagements of the day.

Key actions:
- House-to-house clearance in Be'eri
- Extraction of wounded civilians under fire
- Sustained engagement with Hamas Nukhba forces

## Casualties

Shaldag suffered fatalities at Be'eri on October 7. Specific numbers remain subject to IDF operational security. Multiple commemorative accounts and after-action analyses exist in the collection.
