# Kibbutz Be'eri

**Tag:** `loc:KibbutzBeeri`
**Date:** October 7, 2023
**Location:** Northwestern Negev, ~4 km from Gaza border
**Coordinates:** 31.3869° N, 34.4953° E

## Overview

Be'eri is one of Israel's largest and most established kibbutzim (~1,100 residents). On October 7, it was one of the most heavily attacked communities. The battle lasted approximately 24 hours. Hamas forces infiltrated the kibbutz at multiple points, conducting systematic house-to-house killings. The IDF Shaldag unit played a central role in the rescue operation.

## Casualties

| Category | Count |
|----------|-------|
| Killed | ~100 |

Be'eri suffered the highest absolute death toll of any single kibbutz on October 7.

## Key Events

- **~06:30** — Hamas infiltration begins; multiple breaches in kibbutz perimeter
- **Morning** — House-to-house fighting; residents barricade in safe rooms
- **Afternoon** — IDF Shaldag unit arrives; rescue operations under ongoing fire
- **~07 Oct night / 08 Oct morning** — Final clearance of Hamas forces

## Units Involved

- `unit:Shaldag` — Primary IDF rescue force; sustained significant casualties
- `unit:Yamam` — Additional CT support
- `unit:Paratroopers` — Ground forces

## The Shaldag Operation

Shaldag (Unit 5101), the IAF's elite special operations unit, responded to Be'eri as one of the first organized military forces on scene. The unit suffered multiple KIA in the rescue operation. The Be'eri engagement became one of the most documented special operations actions of the day.
