# Nova Music Festival (Re'im)

**Tag:** `loc:NovaFestival`
**Date:** October 7, 2023
**Location:** Re'im area, northwestern Negev, ~6 km from the Gaza border
**Coordinates:** 31.3721° N, 34.4654° E

## Overview

The Supernova Sukkot Gathering (Nova Festival) was an outdoor trance music festival held in an agricultural area near Kibbutz Re'im. It was attacked by Hamas gunmen beginning at approximately 06:30 on October 7, 2023, after the initial rocket barrage. Approximately 3,500–4,000 young Israelis were present.

## Casualties

| Category | Count |
|----------|-------|
| Killed | ~364 |
| Abducted to Gaza | ~40 |
| Wounded | hundreds |

The Nova Festival was the single largest mass-casualty site of the October 7 attack.

## Key Events

- **~06:30** — Rocket sirens begin; attendees take cover in roadside shelters
- **~06:45** — Hamas gunmen breach the site via paragliders and ground infiltration
- **~07:00–10:00** — Systematic killing and abduction; attendees flee by car on Route 232
- **Route 232 ambush** — Many fleeing vehicles ambushed on the road; mass killing in cars
- **~10:00+** — IDF and Yamam forces begin response; site secured over many hours
