# 🇮🇱 October 7 — Research Archive

A structured research archive of primary and secondary sources documenting the Hamas attack of October 7, 2023, and the Israeli military response. Sources are drawn from a curated [Readwise Reader](https://readwise.io/) collection of 816 documents in Hebrew and English.

---

## Collection Overview

| Tag | Count | Description |
|-----|-------|-------------|
| `October_7_2023` | 816 | Master collection — all sources |
| `Hamas` | 331 | Hamas operations, planning, tactics |
| `Hezbollah` | 77 | Northern front, Hezbollah involvement |
| `🔍Inquiry` | 61 | Post-event investigations and commissions |
| `🚫Gaza23 Locations` | 33 | Location-specific accounts |
| `🟢Gaza21` | 4 | Pre-2023 Gaza conflict documents |

**Languages:** Mixed Hebrew and English. Hebrew-language sources include *Yedioth Ahronoth*, *Haaretz*, *Kan*, *Bamahane*, IDF official releases, and Knesset inquiry transcripts.

> **Note on date metadata:** Many Hebrew-language articles in Readwise display corrupted publication dates (e.g., "Oct 5th 360") due to Hebrew calendar parsing. Dates in this archive are manually verified where possible.

## Methodology

**Source selection:** All 816 documents were collected in Readwise Reader between 2023–2025 from Israeli, Palestinian, and international media sources, IDF official releases, Knesset inquiry transcripts, academic papers, and journalistic investigations.

**Day-of identification:** Articles covering events on October 7, 2023 itself were identified by content analysis (title, summary, source domain) rather than publication date metadata, which is unreliable for Hebrew-language sources in Readwise due to Hebrew calendar parsing errors.

**Tag conventions:**
- `loc:SiteName` — geographic location of attack
- `unit:UnitName` — military/security unit involved
- `DayOf_Oct7` — published on or covering the day of the attack
- Existing Readwise tags retained: `Hamas`, `IDF`, `SOF`, `Inquiry`, `Barbarism`, `Tunnels`, `Drones`, `Hezbollah`, `Warfare`
